How We Work

A deliberate process.
Exceptional results.

Our four-phase engagement model is designed to eliminate guesswork, maximise alignment, and deliver creative that exceeds expectations — consistently.

  1. 01

    Discover & Define

    We begin with a deep-dive into your brand, market, competitors, and audience. We ask the questions others skip. This phase produces a strategic creative brief that every downstream decision maps to.

  2. 02

    Concept & Create

    Our senior creatives develop original concepts grounded in strategy. We present two distinct creative directions — not endless iterations — so decisions are clear, considered, and informed.

  3. 03

    Refine & Perfect

    With a chosen direction, we refine to precision. Copy, colour, spacing, content — every detail is scrutinised. We don't ship work we're not proud of. Period.

  4. 04

    Launch & Scale

    We deliver production-ready assets, provide onboarding on implementation, and remain available post-launch. For long-term partners, we offer ongoing retainer arrangements.

What kind of clients does Blanc Agency work with?
We partner with ambitious founders, established businesses, and creative visionaries who value craft, strategy, and long-term brand equity over cheap, templated solutions. Our clients are typically in lifestyle, tech, hospitality, fashion, and professional services sectors.
What is the typical investment for a branding or web project?
Every project is scoped individually to match your ambition. We work in the premium tier — clients who understand that world-class creative is an investment, not an expense. Reach out to discuss your project and receive a tailored proposal.
How long does a brand identity or website project take?
A full brand identity project typically takes 4–8 weeks. A bespoke website design and development engagement runs 8–16 weeks depending on scope. We prioritise quality and intention — we don't rush craft.
Does Blanc Agency work with international clients?
Yes. While we're based in Kuala Lumpur, Malaysia, we collaborate with clients globally. Our remote workflow is refined and seamless — timezone differences have never been a barrier to producing outstanding work.
What makes Blanc Agency different from other creative agencies?
We are a boutique studio — not a production factory. Every project is led by senior creatives, every decision is intentional, and every output is built to endure. We don't do generic. We build iconic.
Do you offer ongoing retainer arrangements?
Yes. After an initial project, select clients can engage us on a monthly retainer for continued brand management, content creation, and digital growth support. Retainer relationships are limited to ensure service quality.
