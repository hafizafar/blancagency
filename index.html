Health Tech Referral System Community Tool

HIV Risk Assessment & QR Referral Platform

A confidential, mobile-first questionnaire for a high-risk focus community. Participants complete a risk assessment, book a blood test, and — upon completion — receive three unique QR codes to pass on to friends. Each referral unlocks a RM 50 reward for the friend, creating a peer-driven health-awareness loop.

  • ✔ Branching questionnaire with personalised risk scoring
  • ✔ Automated QR code generation per participant
  • ✔ Real-time referral tracking dashboard
  • ✔ End-to-end confidentiality by design
